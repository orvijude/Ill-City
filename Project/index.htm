Ill City

Ill City was established in 2014 with the aim to promote our hometown, Iloilo City, through urban street fashion. Iloilo is the city of love and we wanted to provide Ilonggos with a medium for displaying their love for the city. Our designs showcase some of the city's historic monuments that symbolize how significant our South Town is. Ill City Clothing is jam packed with mean, not just style.
